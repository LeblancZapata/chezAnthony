Our Story

Founded with local flavors in mind, chezAnthony began as a small stall in Yaounde and grew into a neighbourhood favourite, serving burgers with African-inspired spices.

We focus on fresh ingredients, locally-sourced produce, and recipes that mix classic burger techniques with West and Central African influences.

Family-run and community-driven, we’re proud to feed families, students, and friends across the city.
